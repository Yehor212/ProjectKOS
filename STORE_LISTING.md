## Full Description

Tofie Play & Learn Adventures is a safe, ad-free educational game designed by child development experts for children ages 2-7.

**27+ Minigames covering essential skills:**

**Math & Numbers**
- Counting Game — Help animal customers at Tofie's Fruit Market
- Compare Game — Which group has more?
- Pattern Builder — Create bead necklaces with repeating patterns
- Math Bingo — Solve equations on a treasure map
- Cash Register — Run Tofie's shop with real coins
- Math Scales — Share food fairly among friends
- Analog Clock — Learn daily routines and time

**Shapes & Spatial**
- Shadow Theater — Match animals to their moving shadows
- Shape Sorter — Build rockets, boats, and houses
- Size Sort — Family photo day (big, medium, small)
- Magnetic Halves — Free animals from magic mirrors
- Safe Maze — Guide animals through enchanted trails

**Logic & Problem Solving**
- Sorting Game — Help lost animals find their way home
- Odd One Out — Find the photo crasher
- Knight's Path — Navigate the magic forest
- Algo Robot — Program your robot friend

**Creativity**
- Smart Coloring — Paint animals back to life
- Color Lab — Mix magic potions
- Sensory Sandbox — Neon drawing studio
- Forest Orchestra — Conduct an animal concert
- Color Pop — Underwater fish rescue

**Life Skills**
- Weather Dress — Dress Tofie for the weather
- Hygiene Game — Bath time fun
- Eco Conveyor — Save planet Earth by sorting
- Food Game — Feed hungry animals at the restaurant

**Features:**
- 19 adorable animal characters with emotions
- 4 languages: English, Ukrainian, French, Spanish
- Toddler mode (2-4): gentle, no penalties, guided play
- Preschool mode (4-7): progressive challenges, star rewards
- Parental zone with progress tracking
- ZERO ads, ZERO tracking, ZERO data collection (COPPA compliant)
- Works offline — no internet required
- Encrypted save data

**Designed for little hands:**
- Large touch targets (80-120px)
- Max 3-4 choices per screen
- Visual-first design — playable without reading
- Calm animations, no flashing
- Session timer for healthy screen time
