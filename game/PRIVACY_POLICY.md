# Privacy Policy — Tofie Play & Learn Adventures

**Last updated:** 2026-02-22

## Overview

"Tofie Play & Learn Adventures" is a children's educational game designed for kids ages 2-7. We take your child's privacy very seriously.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.** Specifically:

- No personal information (name, email, address, phone number) is collected
- No location data is collected
- No photos, videos, or audio are recorded
- No advertising identifiers are used
- No analytics data is sent to external servers
- No cookies or tracking technologies are used

## Data Storage

All game progress (scores, coins, unlocked items) is stored **locally on your device only**. This data:

- Never leaves your device
- Is not shared with any third party
- Is not accessible to us or anyone else
- Can be deleted by uninstalling the app

## Network Access

This app makes **zero network requests**. It functions entirely offline with no internet connection required.

## Third-Party Services

This app does **not** integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs including:

- No advertising networks
- No analytics platforms
- No social media integrations
- No in-app purchase services

## Children's Privacy (COPPA / GDPR-K)

This app is designed to be compliant with:

- **COPPA** (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)
- **GDPR** Article 8 (Conditions applicable to child's consent)
- **Apple App Store** Kids Category requirements
- **Google Play** Designed for Families program requirements

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or applicable age in your jurisdiction).

## Parental Controls

The app includes a parental gate (3-finger 2-second hold) for features that access settings, the device clipboard (save export/import), and the parent zone.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
