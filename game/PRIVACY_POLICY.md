# Privacy Policy — Tofie Play & Learn Adventures

**Last updated:** 2026-04-04

## Overview

"Tofie Play & Learn Adventures" is a children's educational game designed for kids ages 2-7. We take your child's privacy very seriously. This app is designed to comply with COPPA, GDPR, and all applicable children's privacy laws.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.** Specifically:

- No personal information (name, email, address, phone number) is collected
- No biometric data (face geometry, voiceprints, fingerprints) is collected
- No location data is collected
- No photos, videos, or audio are recorded
- No advertising identifiers (IDFA/GAID) are used
- No device identifiers are accessed or stored
- No analytics data is sent to external servers
- No cookies or tracking technologies are used
- No push notifications are sent

## Data Storage

All game progress (scores, unlocked items, settings) is stored **locally on your device only** in an encrypted file. This data:

- Never leaves your device
- Is not shared with any third party
- Is not accessible to us or anyone else
- Is encrypted using a randomly generated key stored on-device
- Is completely deleted when the app is uninstalled

## Network Access

This app makes **zero network requests**. It functions entirely offline with no internet connection required. No data is transmitted to any server, ever.

## Third-Party Services

This app does **not** integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs including:

- No advertising networks
- No analytics platforms
- No social media integrations
- No crash reporting services
- No cloud storage or sync providers
- No payment processors accessible to children

## Children's Privacy (COPPA / GDPR-K)

This app is designed to be compliant with:

- **COPPA** (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act), including the April 2026 amendments
- **GDPR** Article 8 (Conditions applicable to child's consent)
- **Apple App Store** Kids Category requirements
- **Google Play** Designed for Families program requirements

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction).

## Parental Controls

The app includes robust parental controls:

- **Parental gate:** A cognitive challenge (number puzzle) protects access to settings and the parent zone
- **Exit gate:** A 3-finger 2-second hold prevents accidental app exits
- **Session timer:** Configurable 15-60 minute play sessions (default 20 minutes) with gentle break reminders
- **Save export/import:** Available only in the parent zone via clipboard (no network transfer)
- **External links:** All links to websites (privacy policy, app store) require passing the parental gate

## Parental Rights (COPPA §312.4(d))

As a parent or legal guardian, you have the right to:

1. **Review** your child's information: All game data is stored locally on the device. You can view game progress in the Parent Zone (behind the parental gate).
2. **Delete** your child's information: Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all stored data. No server-side data exists to delete.
3. **Refuse further collection:** Since we collect no personal information, there is nothing to refuse. If this ever changes, we will obtain verifiable parental consent before any collection begins.
4. **Opt out of future contact:** We do not contact users. No email, no push notifications, no in-app messages.
5. **Request information about our practices:** Contact us at the email below. We will respond within 72 hours.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the updated version will be included with the app and posted at the URL below with a new "Last updated" date. We will not make material changes to how we handle children's data without providing notice.
